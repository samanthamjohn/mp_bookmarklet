Item Description

Write a Good Headline.

Sample: 60s Long BOHO Prairie Pleated RUFFLE Maxi DRESS S

No need to write in "vintage" or "vtg" in your headline, because everything is vintage! Use keywords to describe your item. For example...

Era: 20s, 30s, 40s, 50s, 60s, 70s, 80s, 90s.

Style: boho, chic, denim, disco, military, ethnic, avant garde, glam, grunge, gypsy, hippie, mod, punk, rockabilly, pinup, hawaiian, art nouveau, abstract, nautical, etc.

Item Type: dress, coat, heels, handbag,etc.

Brands: List it if it's a designer piece or by a recognized manufacturer.

Features: Include color, material or any specific details that make your item unique: long, pleated, ruffled, bows, big buttons, drop waist, poet sleeves, mini-dress etc.

Description Checklist

General Description: Everything and anything helps! Highlighting color, material, brand, and any features that make your item unique or special. Is it lined? Are the buttons special? Does it have a zipper or elastic waist? Does it come a with matching belt?

Measurements: For best results, include as many measurements as you can.

For clothing, we recommend shoulders, bust, waist, hips, length and sleeve length.

For shoes, list size marked, width marked, insole length, insole width, heel height, boot shaft height and boot shaft opening circumference.

Condition: List any flaws your item may have: holes, stains, rips, fading, smells, etc. You must disclose all flaws to the buyer in the description, even if you don't have a photo of the flaw.

Auction Q+A

How do I set a reserve price?
You do not set a 'reserve' price. You can start your auction at any price you'd like, so set it at the minimum your willing to sell that item for.

What if I have multiple items?
If you have multiple items, you must list each item individually. Each listing is for one item only, or items sold as a set or lot.

What time zone is the starting date in?
Market Publique is on New York city time, the Eastern Time Zone (GMT -5)

Who Pays the Shipping Expenses?

You can choose to have the buyer pay for shipping expenses, by specifying the shipping costs on the form, or you can choose to cover the shipping costs by setting them as $0.

You should include any handling charges or insurance charges in the shipping price as well, and specify what the shipping costs include in the shipping terms (tracking, expedited shipping, insurance amount, etc.

How do I edit my country and Zip/Post Code?
We grab your country and Zip/Postal code from the Edit Your Settings page. Adjusting them there will affect all future listings.

Make it Fresh.

Why Would I Do This?

Fresh is a great way to promote your items on MarketPublique.com.

Fresh items are pulled in randomly every time the page is refreshed, more buyers will see your item regardless of when you listed it.

Since items are displayed in a truly randomized way, we cannot guarantee how many times your item will be displayed.
